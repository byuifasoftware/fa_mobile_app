When will I receive my aid?

- All Financial aid is disbursed within the first week of the semester if the student has completed all of the requirements by their deadlines. However, there is no guarantee date for disbursement.

What documents should I turn in?

- Required documents can be found on your Document Tracking page which can be found under the Finance tab on your My.byui account

My documents say theyre missing

- It takes the Financial Aid Office anywhere from 2 to 4 weeks to process verification documents from when they’re turned.

What deadlines should I know of?

- Students should constantly be checking to make sure they are completing all tasks before deadlines. Click here for a list of deadlines.

Is yout aid amount different?

- Students are re-evaluated every year when they fill out a new FAFSA form. If students receive different financial aid awards from previous years, it’s because the government is evaluating them using their new information.

What can parents do to help?

- All needed financial aid information can be found in every student’s my.byui account under their Finances tab. Frequently look at their document tracking, financial aid awards statement, and personal account summary.
