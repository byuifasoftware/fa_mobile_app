PRIVACY POLICY

Brigham Young University Idaho respects the privacy of this mobile app users, and is committed to protecting that privacy. As the BYU-Idaho Financial Aid mobile app evolves, we may update our privacy policy. We encourage you to review it regularly. We do not collect any personally identifiable information other than what you submit and what your browser transmits. Personal information will only be viewed once you have logged into your mybyui account. We will never ask for your social security number through this app.

How Personal Identifying Information You Supply May Be Used:

  • Any information you provide to us through online forms enables us to respond to your requests or to contact you in connection with the functioning of the Financial Aid site.
  • Problems may be diagnosed with our server and to administer the site.
  • The Financial Aid Office may collect summary data of all users, including number of visitors to the site and features most often utilized. No personal identifying information is part of these summary reports.
This application links to other sites with privacy policies that differ from ours. The BYU-Idaho Financial Aid site is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of any sites to which it links. Please feel free to contact us with questions and concerns you may have about our privacy policy and/or copyright practices.
