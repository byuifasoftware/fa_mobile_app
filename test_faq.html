FAQ
What steps should I follow ?

- Fill Out the FAFSA Form

- Check your document tracking within 4-5 business days

- Continue checking your document tracking to see if youre required to submit more documents

- Your award will be processed 6-8 weeks from the time you submitted your last complete document

What do I need to do?

- Fill out the BYU-Idaho Scholarship Application between November 1 and April 1 of the school year previous to the one you want to receive scholarships.

- Visit our website to find information on outside scholarships

What happens to left over aid?

- Students may pick up remaining financial aid from the Bursars Office. This can be used to pay for other school expenses.

What is Verification?

- The Verification process is used to verify the information you put on your FAFSA. Students are randomly selected for verification and if not completed we cant process your financial aid awards.

What do I find on my.byui?

- View Financial Aid Document Tracking: See what documents weve received and what were requesting.

- View Financial Aid Awards: See the awards you qualify for once youve been awarded.

- View Detailed Personal Account Summary: See when your awards are disbursed into your account and what financial aid you have after all expenses have been paid.

How many credits should I take?

- Check your financial aid awards statement to find out how many credits each of your awards require.
